Event Details

Name: Holi : the Festival of Colors

Date: 25 March 2024

Place: Vrindavan

Description: Also known as the festival of colors, Holi (pronounced Ho-lee) is a Hindu religious holiday that celebrates the victory of good over evil and is one of the most well-known festivals in India. The festival celebrates the feelings of love and pure joy with a chaotic countrywide colored powder fight. Holi 2024 falls on March 25th. The main celebrations last 2 days from March 24th. March 24th is the day when most of Holi's religious rituals will take place, such as Holika Dahan — the ritual bonfire. March 25th is the day of the color fights, when the whole country head to the streets to celebrate. Holi is celebrated by Indians all around the world, but being in India during this festival is a truly once-in-a-lifetime chance to experience the country at its most joyous and captivating.
